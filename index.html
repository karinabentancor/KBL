bio

Karina Bentancor Lenzi is a sound artist and researcher based in Montevideo, Uruguay. Her practice spans installation, performance, and recording, exploring the relationships between sound, space, and perception.

Her work often departs from listening as a critical act — attending to the frequencies, silences, and textures that shape inhabited environments. She works with field recordings, algorithmic processes, and light as materials that extend and question what can be heard.
